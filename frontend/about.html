About Bens Trans

Your trusted partner in transportation since 2020

Our Story

Bens Trans was founded with a simple mission: to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation services to communities across Kenya. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted transportation network serving thousands of passengers daily.

We understand that transportation is more than just getting from point A to point B – it's about connecting people to opportunities, families, and experiences. That's why we've built our business on the foundation of reliability, safety, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional transportation services that connect communities while ensuring passenger safety, comfort, and satisfaction at affordable prices.

Our Vision

To be the leading transportation service provider in Kenya, known for innovation, reliability, and outstanding customer service.
